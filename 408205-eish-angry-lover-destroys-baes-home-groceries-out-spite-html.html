Angry Woman Destroys Bae's Home and Groceries Out of Spite

March 2024 · 3 minute read

- Snaps of the damage that was done to a man's property by presumably his lover have been shared online

- In the snaps, the perpetrator basically wasted all her boyfriend's groceries by pouring the milk into a bucket with his shoes and adding dishwashing liquid to his bread spreads

- Locals have had a lot to say about relationships and the distances people are willing to go to for revenge

It's been said before and it will be said again - relationships are not meant for the weak at heart. In a post shared online, presumably, a woman destroyed most of her boyfriend's groceries and some of his clothing.

The snaps that were shared by @cruel_im_ii show that the woman had poured out all his milk into a bucket of shoes with some cornflakes. She also added dishwashing liquid to his margarine and peanut butter. Different food products were strewn on his bed and mixed up with some of his clothes.
