Reality Star Tacha Recounts Painful Experience With Lagos Staff

April 2024 · 1 minute read

Former Big Brother housemate, Natacha Akide better known as Tacha has taken to social media to recount her horrible experience with one of her support staff In Lagos.

The reality star turned influencer via her Instastories shared her experience with a support staff who almost ruined her business.

She narrated how her driver connived with the gateman to steal fuel.

Tacha further advised her friends and fans to be careful of the kind of support staff they keep around them adding that they could ruined their business if care isn’t taken.

Read her post below;

“Everyone needs to be careful the kind of support staff they keep around them.My company employed this driver on the 31st of August after the fromer driver took one way on Lekki Epe way and LASTMA impounded my van.

I know the struggle I went through to get my van back.A new driver was employed and I assumed everything was okay until I got a mail from the management of the property I stay in, thanking me for my stay and wanting to have a conversation with me”, she wrote
