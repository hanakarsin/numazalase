The Prophetess: Director Says Toyin Abraham Once 'Entered into the Spirit' During Rehearsals

June 2024 · 3 minute read

- Toyin Abraham was said to have gone 'into the spirit' for almost an hour during rehearsals for The Prophetess movie

- This revelation was made by the movie's director, Niyi Akinmolayan via Twitter after a fan commended Abraham for her performance in the movie

- The Nollywood actress confirmed the story to be true as she reacted to the tweet

It is clear to everyone by now that Toyin Abraham is one Nollywood film star who puts her blood and sweat in every role she steps into, and a recent post has confirmed this to be true.

Twitter user, Danny Boy, took to his account to comment on the actress' performance in The Prophetess, which he described as 'stupendous'.

The director of the film, Niyi Akinmolayan then responded to the post, revealing that Abraham who played the titular role, had once 'entered the spirit' during a rehearsal, making the crew wait for an hour.
