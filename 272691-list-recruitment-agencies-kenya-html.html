April 2024 · 8 minute read

The Kenyan economy is among the most vibrant in middle and eastern Africa. Part of the reason for this is the country's incredible human capital base. While unemployment rates are still significantly high in Kenya, there are several positives in the Kenyan job sector. The numerous recruitment agencies in Kenya play a huge role in linking job seekers to potential employers. Which are the best agencies to work with today?

The main reason why big companies work with recruitment agencies is the ability to find professionals quickly. Every reliable recruitment agency must have a highly-qualified human resources department that can identify and pick the ideal employee for their client.

What do recruitment agencies do?

A recruitment agency is essentially a middleman between job seekers and employers. The company works on behalf of employers to find suitable candidates and to fill their vacancies with the best possible person.

Do recruitment agencies help you find a job?

Yes, recruitment agencies fill a specific human resource need for their client. Employers need the right people to apply for their jobs, but often they don't have the time to go and find these people themselves. They use recruitment agencies to do this for them.

How much does it cost to hire a recruitment agency?

The fees vary from agency to agency. Typically, the amount ranges from 10-20% of the annual total salary of the hired person.

Are there recruitment agencies in Kenya?

Yes, there are numerous recruitment agencies in Kenya. The top ones include Recours Four Kenya Consultants Ltd, Flexi Personnel Ltd, and Corporate Staffing Services.

Who oversees recruitment agencies in Kenya?

The Kenyan Ministry of Labour and the Institute of Human Resource Management (IHRM) are the main regulatory bodies that oversee the conduct of Kenyan recruitment agencies.

Do recruitment agencies do interviews?

Yes, if the agency thinks you are among the top candidates for a job, they will arrange an interview with you.

How do I know if a recruitment agency is legitimate in Kenya?

First, check to see if the agency is registered with the Ministry of Labour. Second, ask around to see if anyone you know has used the agency's services before and if they were satisfied with the experience. third, check their web presence. Agencies without active websites are at times suspicious.
