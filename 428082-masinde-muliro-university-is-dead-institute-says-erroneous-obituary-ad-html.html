An erroneous obituary ad has been published on a local daily announcing the death of Masinde Muliro University of Science and Technology (MMUST).

The obituary report which originated from the Office of Registrar was meant to notify the public about the passing of university staff, Sammy Munoko Barasa.

Barasa who was a member of the catering department, died on Tuesday, September 21.

"It is with humble acceptance of God's well that we announce the death of Masinde Muliro University of Science and Technoloy that occurred on Tuesday, September 21," it read.

A screenshot of the announcement circulated online attracting the attention of the university's director of corporate communications and marketing who offered an apology to the affected family.

"The Office of the Registrar wishes to clarify, correct, and apologise for the miscommunication which appeared in today's September 30 Daily Nation newspaper...we highly regret the confusion the publication may have caused," the institute said.

MMUST appointments

In other news, TUKO.co.ke recently reported that the university appointed the former Kibabii University deputy vice-chancellor Academics and Students Affairs Solomon Shibairo as the vice-chancellor.

Shibairo held the position in an acting capacity after taking over from Asenath Sigot, who had held the position for nine months.
