Best Fally Ipupa songs and videos

June 2024 · 9 minute read

Fally Ipupa N’simba, is a Congolese singer, dancer, and songwriter. He is also a guitarist, producer, and philanthropist. Born in 1977, people know him by his stage name Fally Ipupa. His musical career started when he was still young. He has since grown to be arguably the biggest name in the modern Lingala scene. His status has allowed him to collaborate with African and international artists as well as participate in Coke Studio. If you love Fally Ipupa songs, then you’ll definitely be impressed with this list of his top tracks.

Before we check out Fally Ipupa’s greatest hits, you may be wondering when he started singing and his early musical career.

When did Fally Ipupa start singing?

As a Roman Catholic, Fally Ipupa used to sing in the church choir, where he discovered his talent. He started singing for small local groups before attracting the attention of bands like the New Covenant in the 1990s. In 1999, he joined the music band Quartier Latin International, a soukous band that was formed by music legend Koffi Olomide in 1986. After leaving the group in 2006, he went on to become a solo artist.

What was the first song by Fally Ipupa?

In 2006, Fally Ipupa released his first ever solo album Droit Chemin. The album was so popular that it sold over 100,000 units. Droit Chemin is the same album that catapulted Fally Ipupa into the music scene. Since then, he has released many other solo albums and collaborated with other artists to make his music a joy to listen to and sing along.
