Celebrated sports presenter Carol Radull prefers to keep her love life private, away from the public eye. However, Radull has confessed that relationships are not easy.

On Sunday, August 8, the lively media personality was the guest on Churchill Show's journey series, where she talked about her career, upbringing, love for sports and marriage.

Asked about her take on marriage, Radull, who divorced her husband a few years ago, said:
