Jesse Meester From '90 Day Fianc' Has a New Girlfriend, But How Does Ex Darcey Silva Feel?

May 2024 · 3 minute read

Jesse from '90 Day Fiancé' has a new girlfriend. Details on who she is, and how the reality star ended things with ex Darcey Silva on the show.

Breakups are more common on TLC's 90 Day Fiancé than long-lasting marriages, and Darcey Silva and Jesse Meester were just one of the many couples from the show to split. Jesse and Darcey starred on Seasons 1 and 2 of 90 Day Fiancé: Before the 90 Days and the pair forged an unlikely relationship that spanned from Connecticut all the way to the Netherlands.

The two ultimately split, and had no problem blaming each other for the issues in their relationship. Darcey went on to appear on the show again, this time with a new man, Tom Brooks, from whom she has also split. Now she's rumored to be filming a fourth season of the show with a new mystery man. 

Jesse still has an active Instagram profile, and he's now showcasing a new relationship. We have all the details on Jesse from 90 Day Fiancé's new girlfriend, and how Darcey reacted.

Jesse from 90 Day Fiancé's new girlfriend's identity revealed:

We hope for Jesse's sake that things with his new lady love go much better than his relationship with Darcey. The reality star has posted photos with a blonde since June of 2019, whom has been identified as Hofit Golan. The 34-year-old Israeli beauty identifies as a TV host, influencer, and model on her Instagram bio. She also has more than 1 million followers on her page, which is an achievement in and of itself. 

They haven't posted photos together since August, so it's not immediately clear if the pair is still together. But, he did just appear on the Tell-All for Season 3 of 90 Day Fiancé: Before the 90 Days .

Jesse surprised his ex (not necessarily in a good way) with an appearance on the show, as Darcey and Tom were also on rocky ground. He refused to be in the same room as his ex and her new beau, making the entire interaction incredibly uncomfortable. He also got into a tiff with cast member Avery Mills, who questioned why exactly Jesse was part of the Tell-All. 

Jesse also claimed Darcey had been stalking him and wants to get back together with him. She said Jesse wa the obsessed one. However, Darcey might have actually moved on already from both Tom and Jesse with mystery man No. 3.

Is Jesse's ex, Darcey Silva, filming another season of the show?

Crazier things have happened on reality television before. TV Season Spoilers first reported the rumors that Darcey was seen filming with a third man on a New York City rooftop bar. 

A Reddit user claimed her new boyfriend is a bartender at said rooftop bar, and a camera crew filmed Darcey with a guy who looked "like a skinnier Tom." 

The woman believed to be Darcey was carrying a Louis Vuitton bag, which is Darcey's signature look. While there's no official confirmation yet that Darcey will appear on the show again, or that she and Tom are officially broken up, we won't be surprised if she is on Season 4. 

On Part 2 of the Tell-All, Tom did note that it was hard for him to see Darcey flirt with other men. Perhaps this rooftop incident was just her way of trying to make Tom jealous.

90 Day Fiancé: Before the 90 Days airs on Sundays at 9 p.m. on TLC.
