Apostle Okikijesu Sends Warning To Ortom, Yahaya Bello, El-Rufai, Others In Fresh Prophecies [Full L

April 2024 · 4 minute read

The General Overseer of Christ Apostolic Miracle Ministry, Apostle Paul Okikijesu, has warned Governor of Borno, Benue, Logo, Kaduna, Katsina, Niger, Bayelsa, Plateau and Jigawa against an impending danger.

In fresh prophecies sent to Naija News, the cleric warned that these stated needs supervision and adequate protection before things get out of hand.

He advised the governors to assemble Christian religious leader for prayers.

Apostle Okikijesu also stated that the judgment of God will come against those conspiring against Nigeria.

Read prophecies below:

“Nigeria needs supervision:

“Thus says the Lord: Nigeria needs supervision; but write this moment down, the powerful person will become weak. Behold My messenger, those who are claiming to be powerful and have good positions, should realize that everything will come to an end in one day.

“Judgment will come upon the conspirators and the perpetrators:

“Thus says the Lord: The judgment of I the Lord will spread to the people that makes the issues of Nigeria to be precarious and unsafe. The secret that made the International Community not to intervene in the issues of Nigeria will be exposed, and the conspirators who are downplaying the issues of the Nation will receive a harsh judgment.

“Thus says the Lord: The people who are downplaying the issues of Nigeria comprises both male and female. They are the ones that are perpetrating injustice in Nigeria, and all of them will be severely judged.

“These States need supervision and protection from August until October 2021:

“Thus says the Lord: This is the fourth time that I am sending messages to Niger, Katsina, Bornu (Maiduguri), Benue, Kogi, Nasarawa, Kaduna, Bayelsa, Jigawa and Plateau States; these States presently need supervision and adequate protection before things get out of hand.

“The President should act before it gets out of hand:

“Thus says the Lord: The President and those in positions of authority should act; before it is beyond the comprehension of the President of Nigeria. These aforementioned States need adequate security; because it is important. These states need fervent prayers during this period from August until October 2021.

“The Governors of these States should assemble Christian religious leaders for prayer:

“Thus says the Lord: These States need serious prayers; so that mysterious events that can make people to weep will not continue to happen. Therefore, people should raise up their voices in prayer and pray seriously for these States; in order to forestall bloodshed/tragic events between August and October 2021.

“Thus says the Lord: Contact the Governors of these States, and inform them to assemble the Christians religious leaders to awake from their slumber and pray for their States and all its environs.

“Monarchs who conspired will be disgraced and punished:

“Thus says the Lord: All the kings/monarchs in these States that have conspired; will be disgraced and punished. It is important to pray fervently for the States during this period; because all these locations need serious prayer.

“Satan plans to waste people’s lives in Abuja, Lagos, and Osun States:

“Thus says the Lord of hosts: All the Governors that I have previously sent messages should act according to My instructions. Most especially these three States/Cities: Abuja, Lagos and Osun States.

“Thus says the Lord: Inform the Governors of these States to provide adequate security at the entry points to their States immediately because Satan is plotting to waste people’s lives in these States.

“Thus says the Lord: Inform everyone that this message is from I, the Almighty God. I Am who Am; I am the God of the Hebrews; who is the God of their forefathers. My name is the God of Abraham, the God of Isaac and the God of Israel. I reside in the belly of human to speak to everyone. Mankind should listen to, I Am who I Am. For this reason, humans should make amendments according to My instructions. I am the Lord and I do not change, says the Lord. I created the heights and the plains. All power belong to Me. Peace be unto you.

“Ezekiel 33: 1-11: Say to them: ‘As I live,’ says the Lord God, ‘I have no pleasure in the death of the wicked, but that the wicked turn from his way and live.’

“Numbers 23:19: “God is not a man, that He should lie, nor a son of man, that He should repent. Has He said, and will He not do? Or has He spoken, and will He not make it good?”
