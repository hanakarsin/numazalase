The call for former President Goodluck Jonathan to join the 2023 presidential race is currently trending on the microblogging platform, Twitter.

The Peoples Democratic Party chieftain since his defeat to the All Progressives Congress and President Muhammadu Buhari in 2015 had stayed away from the political scene.

There had been speculations that Jonathan might dump the main opposition party for the ruling APC ahead of the 2023 general elections.

A former aide to the ex-president, Reno Omokri a few months ago in his reaction to the news described the speculation as false.

He further stated that his principal is 64 years old and throughout his life, he has only been a member of one political party.
