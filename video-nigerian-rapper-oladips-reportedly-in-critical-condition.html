Nigerian Rapper, Oladips Reportedly In Critical Condition

May 2024 · 2 minute read

Popular Nigerian street rapper, Oladips, is reportedly in critical condition over an undisclosed ailment.

Naija News reports that in a video shared online, Oladips was heard asking his friend about his whereabouts, urging him to quickly take him to the hospital.

While the rapper was being rushed to the hospital, his friends around him were heard repeatedly shouting his name, but he failed to respond.

In a last Instagram story before the incident, Oladips called out his friend, Onlyonelifetime, for leaving him alone at his house during a critical period.

He wrote, “onlyonelifetime_Please Where Are You? I Done Call Your Number Tire 1 Talk Say Make Una No Dey Leave Only Me For This House, I Done Talk This Thing Like A Million Times…Not 1010 Even This Period Like This Please Dey Come House Please Tori Olorun.”

In a post via Instagram, his friend said the rapper’s mother opposed the idea of hospital treatment.

Meanwhile, veteran record producer, Olumide Ogunade, popularly known as ID Cabasa, has said Burna Boy’s recent claims of turning down a $5 million offer to perform in Dubai, United Arab Emirates, UAE, because Emirati law prohibits smoking in public, was not valid.

Naija News recalls that Burna Boy, during a rehearsal with his band, Outsiders, claimed that he rejected $5 million to perform in Dubai because he wouldn’t be allowed to smoke weed while there.

In a recent episode of The Honest Bunch Podcast, co-hosted by actor Chinedu Ani Emmanuel, popularly known as Nedu, ID Cabasa was asked, “Burna Boy rejecting $5 million because he cannot smoke in Dubai. Does it mean he has gotten to the point where he is so addicted to it and cannot do anything without smoking?”

In response, ID Cabasa said Burna Boy’s claim could be a media stunt, considering the amount of money involved.
