Kiharu MP Ndindi Nyoro pledges to help Mihadarati hit maker Stivo Simple Boy advance music career

June 2024 Â· 2 minute read

Kibera sensation Steven Otieno alias Stivo Simple Boy is a man lately on the lips of every Kenyan music lovers, with many having evidently embraced his Mihadarati hit.

One of those hit by Stivo's allure and touching story of a man determined to change his environment is Kiharu MP Ndindi Nyoro, a sure bet the rapper has his future in his hands.

The youthful lawmaker having sampled some of Stivo's hit songs has pledged to offer him help in order to advance his promising music career.

Though it was not immediately established what kind of help, whether financial or material, the mheshimiwa's tone spoke volumes.

"Anyone who knows this man, Stevo Simple Boy, aambiwe namtafuta (tell him, I'm looking for him). I'd want to give him some boost in his music career. Talented yet selling so much hope to the downtrodden. We are African and Africa is our Business," he posted on Friday, August 9.

In an exclusive interview with TUKO.co.ke recently, the musician said he hoped to use his God-given talent to fight drug abuse and teenage pregnancy which are major challenges in slums.
