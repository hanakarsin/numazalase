Jonah Hill's Dating History: A Look at the Actor's Relationships

May 2024 · 4 minute read

Jonah Hill has dated accomplished women like Olivia Millar, Sarah Brady, and Gianna Santos. Here's a look at the actor's relationship history.

Actor and comedian Jonah Hill first made waves with 2007's Superbad and has gone on to star in everything from prestige dramas to political satires, even nabbing a couple of Oscar nods along the way.

As such, folks have been interested in learning more about the You People's personal life — including his dating history. Below, we take a look at Jonah's current and past relationships.

Jonah Hill is currently dating Olivia N. Millar, with whom he shares a child.

Since August 2022, Jonah has been linked to Olivia N. Millar, who is co-founder of an online vintage store called Chasseresse. By March 2023, Olivia was spotted with a baby bump and what looked to be an engagement ring (though an engagement wasn't confirmed).

Olivia then gave birth to her and Jonah's baby in May 2023, per People. She and Jonah continue to remain private about their live together.

Jonah Hill dated surfer Sarah Brady for about a year between 2021 and 2022.

Jonah began dating surfer Sarah Brady sometime around August 2021, based on a selfie of the two that she posted online at the time. The following month, Jonah posted about her to his own account, sharing a pic of the two at dinner with the caption "Grateful for you."

The two seemed to enjoy twinning at different events (they wore matching suits to the premiere of his film Don't Look Up and also wore matching minion Halloween costumes).

According to Sarah's Instagram bio, the surfer is also working to become a lawyer. Per her LinkedIn, she studied the anthropology of climate change and human solutions at the University of California San Diego.

Jonah and Sarah reportedly broke up at some point in 2022. Then, in June 2023, Sarah posted several stories to Instagram in which she accused Jonah of emotional abuse. Her stories included screenshots of their alleged texts as well.

Jonah Hill dated Gianna Santos from 2018 to 2020; the two were also engaged.

Jonah was first linked to Gianna Santos in August 2018. According to Gianna's LinkedIn, the Fordham grad has worked as an art director, content manager, and stylist, among other roles.

In September 2019, news broke that they were engaged. But a little over year later in October 2020, multiple reports claimed that the couple had broken up. Details as to why they split were not shared with the public.

Jonah Hill was also romantically linked to a number of other women including Rita Ora, Allie Hoffman, and Isabelle McNally.

Jonah reportedly had a lot of short-lived romances between 2011 and 2014.

He dated investment banker Jordan Klein, who he met in high school, from 2008 to 2011, per Us Weekly.

“[The reason for the split] was probably traveling,” an insider noted at the time. “She would always go where he was. Now that she has a stable job, she can't just pick up and leave.”

After things ended with Jordan, Jonah also dated actor Dustin Hoffman’s daughter, Allie Hoffman, for nine months before calling it quits in September 2012. They also reportedly attended the same high school.

Later that year, Jonah became linked to British songstress Rita Ora. At the time, Rita was dating Rob Kardashian; however, a source claimed that she had cheated on him with Jonah, which ultimately led Rob to dump her.

In 2014, Jonah was also rumored to have briefly dated Alana Haim, of the music group Haim. He also was said to have dated a woman named Isabelle McNally and nutritionist Brooke Glazer that year.

After things ended with Brooke, Jonah remained single for a few years before getting into a relationship with his now ex-fiancée Gianna Santos.
