Cristiano Ronaldo: Juve star acquires new Marbella villa

May 2024 · 2 minute read

- Ronaldo is known to lead a lifestyle of affluence

- To live up to the billing, the Juve star has KSh 15 million on a new holiday villa

- The four-bedroom property in Marbella adds to his incredible property empire

Juventus superstar Cristiano Ronaldo is set to become neighbours with UFC star Conor McGregor after acquiring an incredible KSh 15 million (£1.3million) holiday villa in Marbella, Spain.

The four-bedroom pad adds to the Portuguese soccer legend's vast property empire estimated to be worth KSh 3.4 billion (£27million).
