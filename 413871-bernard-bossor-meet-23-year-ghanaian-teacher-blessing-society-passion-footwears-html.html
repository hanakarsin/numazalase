Bernard Bossor is a trained teacher but has now groomed himself with another useful skill that he is using to impact society.

The 23-year-old is a teacher at Presby Primary School in the Atiwa West District of the Eastern Region in Ghana shared his story TUKO.coke on how he has been using his skills to empower his community.

According to the gentleman, he discovered his passion for making footwear as far back as 2013, right when he finished junior high school and was about to enrol in a senior high school.
